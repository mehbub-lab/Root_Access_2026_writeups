# The_Lithic_Log

## Challenge Description
The challenge presented a log or image named **The_Lithic_Log**, which required identifying a specific geographical location based on visual clues such as vegetation, sun direction, and environmental characteristics.

## Methodology

### 1. Initial Visual Analysis
I started by analyzing the primary image/screenshot. Key features observed:
- **Vegetation**: Specific types of tropical/subtropical trees.
- **Sun Direction**: Based on shadows, I estimated the time of day and the orientation of the scene.
- **Topography**: Flat terrain with specific marshy or coastal characteristics.

### 2. Regional Filtering
I narrowed down the search to South Asia, specifically focusing on the following regions:
- Bengal
- Sri Lanka
- Tamil Nadu
- Kerala
- West Bengal

### 3. AI-Powered Deep Research
To expedite the identification, I utilized **Perplexity AI** and **Google Gemini** in "Deep Research" mode. I provided descriptions of the flora and lighting conditions to these models to filter out regions that did not match the specific ecosystem.

### 4. Pinpointing the Location
After cross-referencing the vegetation patterns (specifically looking for mangrove-adjacent or deltaic species common in the Sundarbans region) and validating the sun's azimuth for the estimated location, I focused on **West Bengal, India**. 

By comparing the screenshot with Google Street View and satellite imagery in suspected areas of West Bengal, I finally found a 100% match.

## Tools Used
- **Perplexity AI**: For deep research and ecosystem filtering.
- **Google Gemini**: For multi-modal analysis of the vegetation and lighting.
- **Google Lens**: Initial reverse image search to identify plant species.
- **SunCalc / ShadowMap**: To verify if the shadow lengths and directions matched West Bengal at the time of the "log".
- **Google Maps / Earth**: For final verification of the location.

## Final Location
The match was found in a specific district of **West Bengal**, which aligned perfectly with the lithic (rock-based) or log-based clues provided in the challenge title.
